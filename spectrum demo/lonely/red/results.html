Chai Tea

a hot cup of chai tea will warm you right up

Chai Tea

Find it at Argo Tea

Stop by Argo Tea in Snell Library and order a chai tea latte. It’s the perfect blend of spices, tea, and milk

Or make one yourself

What you need
  • 1 whole star anise
  • 1 stick cinnamon
  • 2 1/2 cup water
  • 4-5 black teabags
  • 2 cups milk
What to do
  1. bring 1 cup of water to a boil
  2. steep the tea for 15 minutes
  3. add spices and mix
  4. warm milk and add to tea
