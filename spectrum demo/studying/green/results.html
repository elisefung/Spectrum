Maté Laté

try this energizing antioxidant rich tea for some extra caffeine

Find it at Argo Tea

Stop by Argo Tea in Snell Library and order a delicious Maté Laté. The caffeine in maté tea is energizing, invigorating and is packed with vitamins and minerals. Argo Tea creates their own unique blends of signature drinks that are always naturally fresh and healthy. Try it with almond flavor!

Or make one yourself

What you need makes a 12oz Maté Laté
  • loose leaf yerba maté
  • milk of your choice
  • sweetner and spices (sugar, honey, cinnamon, nutmeg)
What to do
  1. brew 1 heaping teaspoon per 8oz of heated water (175°F) for 10 mins
  2. strain and serve brewed yerba maté (leave some space for milk)
  3. add heated milk and stir
  4. sweeten to taste and enjoy!
