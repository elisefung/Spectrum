Bubble Tea

milky iced tea & chewy pearls

Bubble Tea

Find it at Argo Tea

Stop by Argo Tea in Snell Library and order a bubble tea. Refreshing choices of freshly brewed black, green and red tea blended with milk and all natural de coco jelly pearls. Try it with coconut!

Or make one yourself

What you need
  • 1/4 cup boba tapioca pearls per serving
  • 1-2 tea bags per serving, any kind
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • milk, almond milk, or condensed milk
What to do
  1. bring 1 cup of water to a boil
  2. steep the tea for 15 minutes, then let the tea chill
  3. dissolve the sugar in a small amount of hot water to make a sugar syrup
  4. pour the tea over the boba, sugar syrup, and ice
  5. add milk to taste
