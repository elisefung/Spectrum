Mojito

a delicious minty cocktail with a kick of lime

Mojito

Find it at any Dillon’s Bar

If you’re in Back Bay, stop by Dillon’s Bar and order a mojito

Or make one yourself

What you need
  • tonic water
  • a bunch of fresh mint leaves
  • lime
  • sugar
  • rum
What to do
  1. place the mint leaves and slices of lime in a glass
  2. use a muddler to crush the mint and lime to release their flavor
  3. add sugar and mix
  4. add a shot or two of rum
  5. add ice
  6. pour tonic water over ice and enjoy
