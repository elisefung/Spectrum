Sangria

get a little buzz off some yummy sangria

Sangria

Find it at Huntington Wine & Spirits

Buy a nice bottle of sangria or go for a cheap but tasty boxed wine. Try Franzia's Fruity Red Sangria.

Or make it yourself

What you need
  • a lot of red wine
  • fruit like apples, oranges, and grapes
  • ginger ale or sugar
What to do
  1. cut the fruit into small chunks
  2. add the fruit and wine to a large jug
  3. add ginger ale or sugar to taste if you want some extra sweetness
  4. let sit for a few hours in the fridge
